About Moyo Tech

Who We Are

Moyo Tech is Malawi’s leading technology training academy, empowering youth with cutting-edge digital skills to drive innovation and economic growth.

Our Vision

To be Africa’s premier tech education hub, producing world-class developers, engineers, and digital leaders from Malawi.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, practical, and industry-aligned tech training that transforms lives and powers Malawi’s digital economy.
