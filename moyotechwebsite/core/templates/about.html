Who We Are

Moyo Tech is Malawi’s leading technology training academy, dedicated to transforming youth into skilled, ethical, and globally competitive tech professionals.

Founded to make world-class tech education accessible, Moyo Tech delivers hands-on training in software development, AI, cybersecurity, and digital entrepreneurship — tailored to Malawi’s growing digital economy.

With expert instructors, real-world projects, and partnerships with tech hubs like mHub and Andela, we’re building a pipeline of talent that powers innovation — one coder at a time.

Our Vision

To be Africa’s leading tech education hub, producing world-class developers, data scientists, and digital leaders from Malawi who drive innovation and economic growth.

Our Mission

To empower Malawian youth with accessible, practical, and industry-aligned tech training that creates jobs, builds startups, and fuels Malawi’s digital transformation.
