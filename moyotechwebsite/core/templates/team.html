Who We Are

Moyo Tech is Malawi’s premier technology training academy, dedicated to empowering the next generation of skilled, ethical, and globally competitive tech professionals.

We deliver hands-on training in software development, AI, cybersecurity, and digital innovation — designed to meet global standards while solving local challenges. Our passionate team bridges ambition and opportunity, building Malawi’s tech ecosystem one developer at a time.
