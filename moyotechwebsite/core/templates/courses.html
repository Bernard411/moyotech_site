AFTA Graduates Join Malawi Airlines Cabin Crew Program

AFTA

African Flight Training Academy

Lilongwe, Malawi

The African Flight Training Academy (AFTA) is proud to announce that two of our recent Cabin Crew graduates have successfully passed the Malawi Airlines recruitment interviews and have been selected to undergo the airline's professional cabin crew training in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.

This achievement highlights the high standards and global relevance of AFTA's IATA-accredited Cabin Crew program, which equips students with internationally recognized qualifications and practical skills aligned with airline requirements.
